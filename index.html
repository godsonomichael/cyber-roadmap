🔍 Real SPL queries Nigerian SOC employers test in interviews. Knowing SPL = instant credibility in every Nigerian SOC interview.
🔐 Authentication & Failed Login Detection
Find all failed login attempts
index=main sourcetype=WinEventLog EventCode=4625 | stats count by src_ip, user | where count > 5 | sort -count
Brute force detection — 10+ failures in 5 minutes
index=security sourcetype=WinEventLog EventCode=4625 | bin _time span=5m | stats count by _time, src_ip, user | where count >= 10 | rename count as "Failed Attempts"
Successful login after multiple failures (credential stuffing)
index=security (EventCode=4625 OR EventCode=4624) | stats count(eval(EventCode=4625)) as failures, count(eval(EventCode=4624)) as successes by src_ip, user | where failures > 5 AND successes > 0 | eval risk="High"
🌐 Network & Firewall Analysis
Top talkers — highest network traffic
index=network sourcetype=firewall | stats sum(bytes) as TotalBytes by src_ip | eval TotalMB=round(TotalBytes/1048576,2) | sort -TotalMB | head 20
Port scan detection
index=network | stats dc(dest_port) as unique_ports by src_ip | where unique_ports > 20 | eval threat="Possible Port Scan" | sort -unique_ports
🛡️ Threat Detection & Alert Triage
Suspicious PowerShell execution
index=endpoint sourcetype=WinEventLog EventCode=4688 | where match(CommandLine,"(?i)(encodedcommand|bypass|hidden|invoke-expression|iex|downloadstring)") | table _time, Computer, user, CommandLine | sort -_time
User account created outside business hours
index=security EventCode=4720 | eval hour=strftime(_time,"%H") | where hour < 8 OR hour > 18 | table _time, user, Account_Name, Computer
